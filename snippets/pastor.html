Adam Darnell, Pastor

Adam serves as the pastor for South Durham Church. He grew up in Plano, Texas where he became a Christian when he was 12. Adam holds an M.Div. from Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary and a B.A. in Religion from Belmont University. He is passionate about evangelism and spiritual growth into maturity in Christ. Adam spends his spare time doing web development and playing guitar. He married his college sweetheart, Heather in 2006. They have one son, Nathaniel, and two cats.
