Part-Time Worship Director

The Role

We're looking for a godly man who loves leading people in the musical worship of Jesus Christ. The right candidate will be passionate about the Lord and about developing people for ministry. He'll be a self-starter and constant learner when it comes to his craft, whether he leads from guitar, keys, or an organ. And he can expect to be poured into and cheered on by a pastor who understands the unique challanges of leading worship from personal experience.

This is a part-time paid position. The candidate must be available for the equivalent of one day (planning and discipling) and one evening (rehearsal) during the week in addition to leading on Sunday mornings.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

We help people find the joy they were made for by connecting them to God, each other, and the world through Jesus Christ.

Mission

We exist to make disciples of Jesus Christ to the glory of God.

Our Mission Field

Southern Durham, North Carolina, is in one of the fastest growing regions in the country. It is uniquely diverse, with no majority ethnic group (45% African American, 43% Caucasian) and large south Asian and Middle Eastern populations. By day, it's a thriving shopping hub for the Triangle. By night, it's home to families connected to Duke, UNC, Research Triangle Park, and downtown Durham businesses.

Core Values

God-Centered Worship: God is worthy of all of our worship and all of our praise, on Sundays and throughout the week.

Gospel-Driven Discipleship: The gospel, the good news of Jesus Christ, is not only the way a person becomes a Christian; it's also how Christians grow in holiness.

Grace-Filled Mission: God has lavished his grace on us in Christ so that we would extend his grace to others in gospel proclamation and service.

Our Team

South Durham Church is led by a plurality of elders (pastors) and served by support staff and volunteer Ministry Team Leaders.

We love Jesus passionately because he first loved us, and we see ourselves servants of the saints, always seeking their spiritual growth and faithfulness.

We work together and strive for excellence in all we do, because we work for Jesus.
