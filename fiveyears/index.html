5th Anniversary Celebration

Come celebrate what God has done in the life of South Durham Church!

Join us on Saturday, May 20, 2017
for a party at Sandling Beach
at Falls Lake State Recreation Area.

There will be food, games, swimming, a baptism, and more!

Who:

South Durham Church and friends (please bring friends!)

What:

A fifth anniversary celebration picnic

Where:

Shelter #2 at Sandling Beach at Falls Lake State Recreation Area 14605 Creedmoor Rd. Wake Forest, NC 27587. Click here for a map. Register for the event and we will email you a parking voucher that you can print and bring for free entry.

When:

11 a.m. to 4 p.m. Saturday, May 20, 2017

Schedule

11 a.m. Arrive
11:30 a.m. Lunch served
12:30 p.m. Celebration ceremony
1 p.m. Fun times (Games, volleyball, boating, kids craft, swimming, and more!)
3 p.m. Baptism at the beach
4 p.m. Teardown and depart
